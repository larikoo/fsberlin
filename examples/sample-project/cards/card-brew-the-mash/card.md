Mash temperature target: 65 °C for 60 minutes for a balanced wort.

Use 5 kg of pale malt, 1 kg of Munich, 200 g of crystal 60L. Water
treatment per the recipe sheet.

## Notes
- Strike water at 73 °C accounting for grain temp.
- Check pH at 15 min; adjust with lactic acid if above 5.6.
